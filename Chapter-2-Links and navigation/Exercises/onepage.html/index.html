Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a thrilling Netflix series set in the 1980s. It follows a group of kids as they uncover supernatural mysteries in their small town of Hawkins, Indiana. With government experiments, an alternate dimension called the Upside Down, and a telekinetic girl named Eleven, the series has become a massive hit worldwide.

Cast & Creative Team

Created by: The Duffer Brothers

Main Cast: Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, David Harbour, Winona Ryder, Gaten Matarazzo, Caleb McLaughlin, Natalia Dyer, and more.

Key Characters

  • Eleven - A girl with psychokinetic powers
  • Mike Wheeler - A brave and loyal friend
  • Jim Hopper - The town’s chief of police
  • Will Byers - The boy who vanishes into the Upside Down
  • Joyce Byers - Will’s determined mother

Reviews

Stranger Things has received critical acclaim for its nostalgic 80s references, compelling storytelling, and outstanding performances, especially by its young cast. It holds a 93% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes.
